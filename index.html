Recipe #1: Spaghetti and marshmellows

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: depending on amount of noodles
Ingredients Quantity
  • marshmellows
  • Spaghetti
  • water
  • pot
  • condiments (optional)

    • Step 1: put pot on the stove and add water to half of the pot

    Step 2: put fire on and wait 30 minutes. add some salt for extra flavor

    Step 3: rinse water off pot and pour noodles on a plate

    Step 4: get marshmellows and put each marshmellow into each spaghetti string

    Step 5: grab fork to pick up marshmellow and twirl the noodle around the marshmellow
